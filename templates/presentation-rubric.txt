RUBRIC SCORING GUIDE:

EXCELLENT (90-100): Outstanding performance that exceeds expectations
- Exceptional knowledge and preparation
- Engaging and professional delivery
- Creative and effective visual aids
- Excellent audience interaction

GOOD (80-89): Proficient performance that meets expectations
- Good understanding of topic
- Clear and confident delivery
- Well-designed visual aids
- Appropriate audience engagement

FAIR (70-79): Developing performance with room for improvement
- Basic understanding of topic
- Adequate delivery with some issues
- Simple but functional visual aids
- Limited audience interaction

POOR (Below 70): Performance needs significant improvement
- Limited understanding of topic
- Unclear or uncomfortable delivery
- Poor or missing visual aids
- Minimal audience engagement
