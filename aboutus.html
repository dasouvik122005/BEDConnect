About B.Ed Connect

Welcome to B.Ed Connect - the Department of Education at JIS University, one of the top B.Ed courses in Kolkata. The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) is a professional degree program that equips aspiring educators to teach at the middle level (Classes VI-VIII), secondary (Classes IX-X), and senior secondary levels (Classes XI-XII).

Our Mission

To develop competent, committed, and compassionate teachers who can transform the educational landscape through innovative pedagogy, strong ethical values, and a deep understanding of diverse learning needs. We aim to create educators who inspire, engage, and empower the next generation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in teacher education, fostering a community of lifelong learners who contribute meaningfully to society. We envision producing educators who are not just teachers, but mentors, researchers, and change-makers in the field of education.

JIS University offers a standout program designed to blend academic theory with practical classroom experience. We focus on giving teacher trainees hands-on exposure to modern teaching methodologies, digital tools, and innovative learning strategies. The curriculum is thoughtfully structured to instill strong ethical values, a modern outlook, and a sense of responsibility in future educators.

Accreditation & Recognition: Aligned with the guidelines of NCTE (National Council for Teacher Education) and WBUTTEPA (West Bengal University of Teachers' Training, Education Planning and Administration), the B.Ed program at JIS University includes both theoretical knowledge and practical training to ensure comprehensive skill development.

JIS Group: Part of JIS Group Educational Initiatives - the largest educational conglomerate of Eastern India with 37 Institutions, 170 programmes and 39,000 students. With over 24 years of excellence in education, JIS University ranks among the top B.Ed courses in Kolkata.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty members, and strong industry connections, we prepare our students for rewarding careers in schools, educational institutions, and beyond.
