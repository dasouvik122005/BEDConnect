About BEDConnect

Welcome to BEDConnect - the Department of Education at JIS University, a premier institution dedicated to shaping the future of education in India. Our Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) program is designed to transform passionate individuals into skilled, reflective, and innovative educators who will lead the next generation.

Our Vision: To be a center of excellence in teacher education, fostering critical thinking, pedagogical innovation, and inclusive educational practices that meet the evolving needs of 21st-century learners.

Our Mission: We are committed to providing comprehensive teacher training that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical experience. Our NCTE-approved program emphasizes hands-on learning, research-driven pedagogy, and holistic development of teaching professionals.

Accreditation & Recognition: Our B.Ed program is approved by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) and affiliated with the West Bengal University of Teachers' Training, Education Planning and Administration (WBUTTEPA), ensuring that our graduates meet the highest standards of teacher education.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty members, and strong industry connections, we prepare our students for rewarding careers in schools, educational institutions, and beyond.
