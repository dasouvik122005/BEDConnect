Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)

A comprehensive two-year teacher training program designed to develop skilled and reflective educators

Program Overview

The B.Ed program at JIS University is a two-year full-time course approved by NCTE and affiliated with WBUTTEPA. This professional degree program equips aspiring educators to teach at the middle level (Classes VI-VIII), secondary (Classes IX-X), and senior secondary levels (Classes XI-XII). With the rise of new schools every year, the demand for trained, competent teachers is growing rapidly.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's Degree (B.A./B.Sc./B.Com) with minimum 50% marks
  • 45% marks for SC/ST/OBC/PWD candidates
  • Graduation in any discipline from a recognized university
  • For science & mathematics teaching: Relevant subject in graduation
  • Candidates appearing in final year can also apply

Admission Process

  • Fill online application form
  • Appear for entrance examination (if applicable)
  • Participate in counseling process
  • Document verification
  • Fee payment and admission confirmation
  • Seat allotment based on merit
